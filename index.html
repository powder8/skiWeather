Snowpack Evolution

Snowpack Evolution Through the Week:
Warmer temps on Tue, Mar 17, Wed, Mar 18, Thu, Mar 19, Fri, Mar 20 will promote settlement and bonding of recent snow.
Wind events (Tue, Mar 17, Wed, Mar 18, Thu, Mar 19) will create reactive wind slabs on lee features. These can persist for 1-2 days after winds ease.
